Questions

What is GEO?

Generative Engine Optimization — making your business visible to AI answer engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity and Gemini, so when a customer asks "who's the best near me?", the answer is you. It's the successor to SEO for the growing share of buyers who ask AI instead of searching.

How does the scan work?

We put three real buyer questions to AI engines — one branded ("your business, reviews") and two category questions ("best [what you do] in [your area]") — then check whether you're cited, who is, and score it out of 100.

Why don't I show up?

AI engines cite sources they can parse and trust: structured data on your site, consistent business details across the web, reviews, and pages that answer buyer questions directly. Most SME websites were built for Google's old rules — that's fixable, and your report tells you where to start.
